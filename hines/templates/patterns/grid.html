The basic grid structure is:

<div class="grid">
  <div class="grid__row">
    <div class="grid__col"></div>
    <div class="grid__col"></div>
  </div>
</div>

Add modifying classes to .grid__col for different widths etc.

All columns collapse to be full-width from 768px wide down.
